The Peterson Family

From a Minnesota chocolate shop to Mile Marker 100.

Sweets are a family legacy for Bronna and Rich Peterson. Bronna's grandmother owned her own restaurant and bakery — pies, cakes, cookies, the works. Rich's family owned Ramer's Chocolates & Confectionary, a name Minnesotans knew for decades.

In 2010, they brought the recipes south. The flagship shop opened at Mile Marker 100 in Key Largo. Sixteen years later — and a second shop down the road in Islamorada — they're still hand-crafting truffles in the back of the Key Largo shop and pulling Key Lime Pies out of the same family oven.
